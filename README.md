USER STORY FOR NON TRCHNICAL AUDIENCE ABOUT REPUBLIC DASH APP

WHAT THE PRODUCT DOES: It is a smart digital online food ordering and delivery app that allows chicken republic customers order food fast,fresh and in a convenient manner,from anywhere and at anytime. it connect her customers to nearby chicken republic restuarants, allowing them to place and track orders in real time as if they were visiting the store physically.

WHY THE PRODUCT MATTERS: In Nigeria today,customers desire speed, convience and consistency, but long queues in restuarants have made these services unattainable. these re-occuring issues brought about the creation of the app. The CR dash app solves the problem of the following issues.

Reducing Time waiting: customers have the opportunity to order ahead and get the order delivered ontime.
Building Loyalty: Customers get first hand promotions directly from the restuarants and sometimes stand a chance of earning points to reduce meal orders.
Improving the statisfaction of customers: meals are made fresh,traceable and accurate.
Consolidating all other apps into one app: other apps like Glovo and chowdeck are put into one app, to help track payment,orderings and promotions and to help the company track the activities of the other apps.
WHO IS THE PRODUCT FOR: The app was designed for everyone especially people who love the Chicken Republic brand and products. These people are as follows;

Busy Professionals
Families
Students
Franchise and restuarants team
KEY FEATURES: The key features of the app are as follow; 
a. Live ordering
b. secure payment platform 
c. consolidating of all aps 
d. smart ordering 
e. Pickup and ordering 
f. feedbacks and supports 
g. Personalised Menu 
h. No price discrepancies restuarant vs app 
i. Rewards and loyalty.
